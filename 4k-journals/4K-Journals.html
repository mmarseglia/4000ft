4,000 Footer Journals

In June 2,000 Bob Farrell (Farmer Bob) started a site on which peakbaggers could record their progress in doing the various New England lists of peaks. In August 2004 he found that he did not have the time to continue it. His site filled a unique niche in the world of cyber-hiking, so I have decided to continue it, in a modified format. Should you be interested in having your list kept here, please email me.

Recent List Completions by Members

On Thursday, September 23rd, 2004, Lisa Marie Pane (Alpinista) finished the New Hampshire Fours on Mount Isolation, becoming the first member to finish a list since I took the Journals over. Congratulations, Lisa!

Two days later, on September 25th, 2004, Tony Schilling completed the New Hampshire Fours on the two Tripyramids. Congratulations, Tony!

On July 3rd, 2005 Julie Smith (Cantdog) finished the New England 100 Highest list on Fort "in fabulous weather" to quote her email. Congatulations, Julie!

On Saturday, September 17th, 2005, Michael J. Saletnik (MichaelJ) completed the New England Fours on Mount Mansfield. Congratulations, Michael!

On Sunday, September 25th, 2005, Amy DeMelia (Poison Ivy) completed the New England Four Thousand Footers on North Brother. Congratulations, Amy!
