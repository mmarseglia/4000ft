4K Journal for Steve H (PooBear)

Steve H. (PooBear) grew up in England and moved to Boston at the end of 1999 (the hikes listed in 1995 were done on a vacation to this area, going hut to hut). He lives in Somerville, Massachusetts, works in downtown Boston and attends graduate school in the evenings. He is 31 years old. He has been a member since 01/29/05, and has climbed 35/48 New Hampshire Fours, 2/48 in winter.
