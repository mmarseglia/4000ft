4K Journal for Michael Saporito (sapblatt)

Michael Saporito (sapblatt) has been a member since 09/30/2004, and has now climbed all New Hampshire Fours, 62/67 of the New England Fours and 66/100 of the New England 100 Highest. Michael lives with his wife and young son in Peabody, MA and works as a librarian in Boston. He hopes to climb all of the 4000 footers in next couple of years and then hopefully do them all again with his son (but only if he wants to!)
