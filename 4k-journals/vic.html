4K Journal for Vic "Backbeat" Pimentel and Louanne "Stumpling Dear" Blackburn.

Vic "Backbeat" Pimentel and Louanne "Stumpling Dear" Blackburn have been members since 08/27/03 and have climbed 8/48 of the New Hampshire Fours.

Louanne is an RN working at Beverly Hospital. After hiking her next love is working in her extensive gardens, which include a Japanese water garden. Vic is a member of the MA Dept. of Conservation and Recreation K9 Search and Rescue Team with his young Golden, "Mattie" and the Central Mass Search and Rescue Team.
