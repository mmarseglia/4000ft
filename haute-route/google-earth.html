My Itinerary

I have included a Google Earth (.kmz) file for each stage that I have described; here I will list them all in a west to east order, rather than the chronological order in which they were done. Explore them from different angles and at different zoom levels! Try using the "Play Tour" button that is at the bottom of the "Places" panel; some of the tours are clearly better presented than others.

  • From the Lac des Dix to Arolla, spending a night at the Cabane des Dix
  • From Arolla to Les Haudères by way of Lac Bleu and La Gouille.
  • From Evolène to the Col du Torrent, up and down, exploring another route from the Val d'Hérens to the val d'Anniviers.
  • From Evolène to the Cabane de Moiry by the Col du Tsaté.
  • From the Cabane de Moiry to the Sorebois cable car.
  • From the Sorebois cable car to Zinal
  • From Zinal to Gruben
