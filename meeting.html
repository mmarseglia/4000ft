Four Thousand Footer Club Annual Reunion and Awards

The Annual Reunion and Awards Dinner normally takes place in mid or late April. For the past two years it has taken place in Stratham, NH (near Exeter), and I believe that it will be held there next year. When information becomes available it will be posted here.

Please refer to the 4000 Footer Club FAQ for more information about the Club.
